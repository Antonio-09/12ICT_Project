About Us

In OneDot. Coffee, we're a community of coffee lovers committed to giving you the best coffee experience in town. We're more than simply a spot to get your caffeine addiction.

Our Story

OneDot was established by a committed group of coffee lovers. Coffee started as a dream to build a place where people could enjoy a perfect shot of coffee and interact with one another. As time has passed, that dream has transformed into a treasured neighborhood gathering place, where relationships grow and memories are told over warm mugs of carefully brewed coffee.

Our Coffee

We take pride in finding the best coffee beans available abroad. Every espresso shot, pour-over, and cappuccino is meticulously handcrafted by our baristas, each of whom is a talented artisan. We have something to satisfy every coffee connoisseur, whether you prefer a rich and strong roast or a delicate and nuanced blend.
